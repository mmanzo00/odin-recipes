Risotto

Description

Risotto is a creamy Italian rice dish known for its rich, velvety texture achieved by gradually adding warm broth to Arborio rice while stirring constantly.

The process relies on the starch released from the rice to create a luxurious consistency.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Onion or shallot
  • White wine
  • Unsalted butter
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley or chives

Steps

  1. Cook the onions and garlic
  2. Add the rice and wine
  3. Continue to cook the risotto, add the parsley
  4. Serve
