Tiramisù

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert featuring layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich, creamy mascarpone filling, finished with a dusting of cocoa powder.

It is a no-bake dessert that benefits from chilling time to allow the flavors to meld and the ladyfingers to soften.

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese: 16 ounces (450g)
  • Ladyfingers (Savoiardi): 28 to 45
  • Coffee: 1 to 1.75 cups
  • Granulated sugar: 1/3 cup to 2/3 cup
  • Egg yolks: 4 to 6,
  • Egg whites: 2 to 4
  • Cocoa powder: 1/3 to 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Prepare the espresso mixture
  2. Prepare the mascarpone mixture
  3. Prepare the zabaglione (egg yolk mixture)
  4. Combine the zabaglione and mascarpone
  5. Whip the cream
  6. Assemble the tiramisu
  7. Dust with cocoa
  8. Chill
