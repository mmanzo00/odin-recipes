Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian baked pasta dish made with wide, flat sheets of pasta layered with various fillings and sauces, then baked until golden and bubbly.

It is a staple of Italian cuisine, with regional variations across the country, and is known for its rich, savory layers and comforting texture.
